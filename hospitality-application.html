Product Introduction
Everstar CCT Tunable COB is a chip-on-board LED module that enables independent adjustment of brightness and CCT, this technology provides the ability to control the color temperature (2700K to 6000K) of a LED luminaire. Ideal for human-centric architectural lighting applications, the 2-channel design provides high-quality white light without sacrificing efficacy.

Features
Two-channel CCT changing
Consistent white light
High lumen density for directional lighting
Accurate color mixing
Application
Human-centric lighting
Hospitality lighting
Museum lighting
Multi-function space lighting
Architectural lighting
Track lighting
