Product Introduction
Everstar 3535 ultraviolet series is a highly reliable and grade ceramic substrate, which is widely applied to sterilization and purification in the field of industrial and medical applications with a very low calorific value and a high optical power. Its light efficacy is up to 12.0 mW with higher forward currents.

Features
High reliability
Low thermal resistance
Standard surface-mount packages
Easy integration
Application
Surface disinfection
Appliance sterilization
3D Printing
Maskless lithography
