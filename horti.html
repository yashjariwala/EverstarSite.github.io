Product Introduction
This part has a footprint that is compatible with most of the same-size LEDs in the market today. This product is designed for flowering and fruiting plants and can be used for most shade and neutral flowering plants as well as for fruiting plants. Adding a small amount of green light can increase the lighting effect. The photosynthetic photon flux efficiency (At 1W) > 2.3umol/J can meet the requirements of promoting plant growth. Higher PPF/W recommended at 0.5W.

Features
High luminous intensity and high efficiency
Compatible with the reflow soldering process
Low thermal resistance
Long operation life
Wide viewing angle at 120°
Application
Flat-panel light
LED tube light
LED bulb light
Horticulture light
